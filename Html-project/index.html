About our gallery


Tradition since 1923

As technology has exponentially evolved in the past decade, it has greatly impacted people’s daily life in different ways. However, the desire for stationery products with exceptional Quality, Uniqueness, Creativity, and Beautiful Design has always remained inside the heart of every stationery lovers out there.

Fountain Pen Gallery is an online store based in Toronto, Canada. It was founded on July 18, 1923, by three stationery enthusiasts, Josiane, Paulo, and Roselane, with the desire to bring to you all the top Quality and Interesting Fountain Pen products around the world. With over 100 years of dealing with Canadian companies through Fountain Pen Gallery work, our works impression is amazing for giving as a gift on special occasions.
